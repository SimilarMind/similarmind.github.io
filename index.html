About Similar Mind

Florent Agostini was born in Beaumont in the Puy-De-Dome and received his baccalaureate in 2010 at Clermont-Ferrand. Having explored small amateur film projects since a young age, he goes off to ESEC to study cinema as soon as he turns 18.

He then decides to leave university after his first academic year in order to work on a telefilm, "Les Anonymes", directed by Pierre Schoeller. By chance, he obtains the position of broadcast technician for the television channel BFM TV, which allows him to forge his outlook onto the wider world. This job relatively far from the 7th art, liberating occasionally considerable frustrations, became his largest source of determination. Florent Agostini creates in 2015 the Label SIMILAR MIND, name under which he kick-starts the writing of his first short film, SIMILAIRES.
